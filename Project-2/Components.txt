React Component Structure for Your Project

1] Header.jsx – Contains navigation links and branding.
         
2] Intro.jsx – Brief introduction to the website or project.

3] Flamingo.jsx – Needs clarification on purpose.

4]Experience.jsx – Highlights experience and achievements.

5] ClientReview.jsx – Displays client feedback and testimonials.

6] Details.jsx – Provides additional information or FAQs.

7] Footer.jsx – Contains links, contact details, and social media.
